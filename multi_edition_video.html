The video below shows improvements on the multi-edition and code-completion for the LiClipse HTML editor.

Multi-edition below is activated with Ctrl+K (forward search) or Ctrl+Shift+K (backward search).

Alternatively, one can use Ctrl + Alt + double-click a word to add it to the multi-edition or Ctrl + Alt + Click with a selected region.

A selected region can be unlinked with K
