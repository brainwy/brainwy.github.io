Note that LiClipse is still only available for Beta testers that got related perks on its Indiegogo campaign but it should be available for everyone shortly.

The video below shows improvements on the multi-edition and code-completion for the LiClipse HTML editor.

Multi-edition below is activated with Ctrl+K (forward search) or Ctrl+Shift+K (backward search) and a region may be removed from the selection with Alt + Shift + K.

Alternatively, one can use Ctrl + Alt + double-click a word to add it to the multi-edition or Ctrl + Alt + Click with a selected region..
